Released our first game: NiceFart.io
Mar 17, 2017
1 minute read

We’re so happy today because our first game is online now!
NiceFart.io is a online multiplayer game for browsers based in a hilarious world where the players are butts trying to beat other players eating beans, and shooting farts.

This game is similar to agar.io or slither.io, games where your goal is eat and become the biggest player. For now, the game is available for browser, but we’re working hard to have soon versions for Android and iOS.

We made NiceFart.io with love, and we hope you enjoy it!
